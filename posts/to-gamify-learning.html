To Gamify Learning

Games are fun, so why can't learning be?

  2. The Outcome

When Skillweaver was first starting to progress, I couldn't help but think about how the student would use the platform. Although Skillweaver was made to support teachers, I couldn't help but think about how to get students to enjoy the learning process.

The Idea Behind Using Gamified Learning

Games are a fun pass time to most people, well not actually, the real reason games are fun is because of the challenge they provide. When students are challenged, and a goal is set, to be acheived students will be more likely to engage with the learning process. It is like beating a level and still having to learn how to beat the next level.

The Outcome

Skills, progression and mastery. The perfect combination for moving forward and keeping students engaged, while also providing instructors the feedback that they need to know where a student is struggling. Seeing the feature working, made me really happy because as someone who does enjoy games, I feel like when it gets added to a learning environment, it will make the learning process more engaging and enjoyable.
